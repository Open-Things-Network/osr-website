Tasks

We want to reach our goals through the accomplishment of tasks listed below. However it isn't closed list - we count on the active participation of the community in their verification and proposing other, perhaps those better accomplishing our goals.

  1. Active participation in decentralization of The Things Network by creating and maintaining our own TTN public network cluster in Poland.
    • benefits:
      • the ability to independently solve problems related to the functioning of the network - depends on us and in a shorter time,
      • lower data transmission delays,
      • data remains in the country,
    • duties:
      • acquiring the adequate knowledge,
      • taking care of resource availability and security,
      • organizing technical support.
  2. Popularization of LoRaWAN technology and the broadly understood Internet of Things by participating in events related to science and technology, conducting lectures and workshops, and organizing conferences.
    • organization of TTN On Tour conference - meeting with project initiators,
    • technology publications, promotion of projects and solutions using LoRaWAN, equipment testing, etc.
  3. Creating the catalogue of Polish companies handling LoRaWAN technology.
