Tasks

We want to reach our aims through the accomplishment of exchanged below objectives. However it isn't closed list - we count on the active participation of the community in their verification and proposing other, perhaps of subjects better accomplishing these purposes.

  1. Active participation in the decentralization of The Things Network by creating and keeping the own cluster of the TTN public data network in Poland.
    • Benefits:
      • the possibility of solving the problems connected with working of networks associated with functioning on one's own - dependent on us and in the shorter time,
      • smaller delays of the data transmission,
      • data remains in the country,
    • duties
      • acquiring the adequate knowledge,
      • caring about the availability of resources and the safety,
      • organization of the technical support.
  2. The popularization of the LoRaWAN technology and the widely comprehended Internet of Things through the participation in events connected with the science and technology, leading the talk and workshops and organising the conferences.
    • organisation of TTN On Tour conference - meeting with initiators of the project,
    • the publications concerning the technology, promoting projects and using LoRaWAN solutions, testing the equipment,
  3. Creating the catalogue of Polish companies dealing with the LoRaWAN technology.
