Aims

  1. Building the open network infrastructure appropriate for the Internet of Things in Poland for creating professional applications, as well as to testing purposes and the prototyping.
  2. Supporting the network open infrastructure and connected with it the ecosystem which can create building the ideas from area of the Internet of Things.
  3. Promotion of cooperation, with colleges, universities, individuals researches point sand public administration in propagating and using new technologies, with the Internet of Things.
