Manifesto

We see the huge potential of the Internet of Things and we want Poland, including local communities, to be able to use this potential.

We believe that easy access to the Internet of Things infrastructure, which does not require license and subscription fees, can be a crucial factor supporting communities, scientific institutions and startups, helping them compete in the modern market.

We think that creating the open infrastructure can be a catalyst for new ideas (both open and commercial) for applying the idea of the Internet of Things in various areas.

We believe that, as a consequence, new initiatives and ideas will have a positive impact on the quality of life, safety and health of the community and individuals.
