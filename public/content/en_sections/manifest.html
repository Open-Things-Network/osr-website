Manifesto

We notice the enormous potential of the Internet of the Things and we want Poland and the local communities could also exploit this potential.

We think, that simple access to infrastructure for the Internet of the Things which doesn't require royalties and licence can be a crucial factor supporting communities, research institutions and startupy, helping them to compete on the contemporary market.

We think that creating the open infrastructure can act as a catalyst for new ideas (both opened and commercial) for applying the idea of the Internet of Things in various areas.

We believe that in consequence new initiatives and ideas will be having a positive effect on a quality of life, a safety and a health of the community and individual person.
