July 6, 2020 2020-07-06

Version 1.1 of our LoRa development board had its official premiere on June 21 during Arduino & Raspberry Pi Day 2020.

The printed circuit board, whose authors are members of our association, offers a lot of configuration options, such as the choice of a transceiver module (RFM95W, RN2483A), or the use of various power methods (USB, battery, solar panel).

The circuit board design with full documentation and sample programs is available for download from GitHub repository.
