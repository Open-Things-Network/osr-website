Version 1.1 of our LoRa development board had its official premiere on June 21, 2020 during ARDUINO & RASPBERRY PI DAY

The board, whose authors are members of our association, offers great configuration options. Among other things, the choice of the used transceiver module (RFM95W, RN2483A) and the use of different power methods (USB, battery, solar panel).

The circuit board design with full documentation and sample programs is available for download from GitHub repository.
