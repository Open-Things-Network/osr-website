January 14, 2020 2020-01-14

The General Meeting of Members of the Association adopted a resolution regarding the amount of membership fees, as well as how to pay them.
Pursuant to the resolution, the amount of the monthly contribution is PLN 20, and the obligation to pay it arises from the first day of the month following admission to the membership of the Association. Contributions should be paid to the organization's bank account by the 20th day of each month. A membership fee payment van be done for any period longer than one month.
