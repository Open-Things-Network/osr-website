The Things Network

What is The Things Network?

The Things Network is a project initiated in 2015 in Amsterdam. Its aim is building a global network for the Internet of Things using LoRaWAN technology, which is characterized by low energy consumption, long range and low bandwidth, so it is ideal for IoT solutions. LoRaWAN technology has gained a great popularity due to the possibility of easy and relatively cheap creation of a network for battery-operated devices. The Things Network project has made this even easier by providing an open network infrastructure - just set up the gateways and focus on the end nodes and applications. The network began to grow very quickly and is already being developed in over 100 countries around the world. Also in Poland, local communities have been created since 2016 to gather enthusiasts interested in network development - mainly in large cities.

TTN network is open, distributed, decentralized, free of charge, based on an open software stack and is owned by the community that creates it.

Open means that everyone has access to it and can contribute to its development. Anyone can increase the network coverage by installing and starting another gateway. The network infrastructure is therefore developed and maintained by its users. The network is distributed because the basic components of the network backend can be run in any domain. Sustainable because it maintains a balance between the number of users using the network and contributing resources to it, and decentralized because it means involving as many parties as possible cooperating with each other to operate and develop the network.

Why TTN?

  1. Knowledge and development
    • a project permanently associated with LoRaWAN technology
      • competence center related to this technology
      • The Things Conference, LoRaWAN Academy, active participation in creating future versions of LoRaWAN standard
    • an incubator of interesting ideas and projects
    • the largest and growing number of users
      • easy contact with users from various regions of the world
      • sharing knowledge and experience through the forum and community sites.
  2. The software
    • open source code
    • continuous development and quality improvement
    • ready integrations with popular platforms allowing for quick creation of new solutions and focusing on the problem space, not on problems with network infrastructure.
  3. Architecture
    • scalable and distributed
    • decentralized
    • ensures high availability and performance as well as compliance with LoRaWAN specification.

TTN communities

Local TTN communities are groups of people from a specific area (mainly cities) who deal with the implementation of The Things Network by building the LoRaWAN network, promoting the idea of an open network, acquiring partners and locations for gateway installation, organizing workshops and meetings. Communities consist of many people, organizations and interest groups. They include technology enthusiasts, representatives of companies and academic circles.

Polish communities involved in The Things Network are part of the global TTN community and operate in accordance with the principles set out in TTN Manifesto.

In 2018, a group of enthusiasts from several TTN communities (Bydgoszcz, Łódź, Ostrów Wielkopolski and Toruń) set themselves the goal of strengthening interest in developing the concept of an open network based on TTN, improving the reach of this network and creating a non-profit organization that would support Polish TTN community and has undertaken cooperation activities to build, maintain and promote an open LoRaWAN network.

Association

The Open Things Network Association was established in December 2019.

The organization is to be a tool supporting and integrating local TTN communities, and not something that will replace them.

We want to represent local communities and support them in achieving a common goal, as well as improve communication between community members. The creation of the association will allow us to talk more easily and effectively with company representatives and institutions, raise funds for achieving goals, and better advertise our ideas and more effectively reach interested persons.

The use of the TTN network, its potential and knowledge as well as cooperation and support of the TTN community is currently the best way to achieve the Association's goals. For this reason, we treat this project as a priority.
